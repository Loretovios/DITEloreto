Bacong

This is a small town in the coast, with the neighboring area of Dumaguete City located on the south. In the Philippines, this is the smallest and fourth class Negros Oriental municipality. The people here are reputed for their heroism since those days when the Moro pirates came to here as attackers. Bacong is categorized into twenty-two varied barangays – that are regulated by their own captain.
